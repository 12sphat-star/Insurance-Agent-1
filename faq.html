Common Questions & Expert Answers

Everything you need to know about protecting your family and building wealth.

Why should I work with an independent agent like Keith?

Unlike "captive" agents who work for one specific insurance company, an independent agent works for you. I have access to dozens of top-rated carriers, allowing me to shop the entire market to find the best possible strategy and pricing for your unique situation.

What is the "Rockefeller Method" of estate planning?

This method involves using a combination of whole life insurance and family trusts to create a perpetual line of credit for the family, similar to how the Rockefeller family has preserved and grown their wealth for generations. It focuses on the "family bank" concept.

Can I really use life insurance for retirement income?

Yes. Specifically structured policies like IUL or whole life allow you to build up cash value that can be accessed via policy loans. These loans are typically tax-free, providing a powerful alternative to traditional retirement accounts that are subject to future tax rates.

Is Infinite Banking safe?

Infinite Banking is a strategy that utilizes dividend-paying whole life insurance from mutual companies that have been around for over 100 years. These companies have survived the Great Depression, the 2008 crash, and world wars while consistently paying dividends. It is widely considered one of the safest financial foundations available.
