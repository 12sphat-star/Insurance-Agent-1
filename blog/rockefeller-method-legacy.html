Most people build wealth to spend it. A few build wealth to pass it on. But the elite build wealth so it never leaves the family. This is the core of the **Rockefeller Method**.

When John D. Rockefeller established his legacy, he didn't just leave a pile of cash. He left a system. A system where the family's assets were protected inside a private board, and every subsequent generation had access to "loans" from the family bank—provided they followed the family's constitution.

Breaking the Cycle of "Shirtsleeves to Shirtsleeves"

There is an old proverb that wealth lasts only three generations. The first generation builds it, the second enjoys it, and the third destroys it. The Rockefeller Method is specifically engineered to break this cycle.

By using **specifically engineered whole life insurance** as the cash reserve, the family bank grows at a guaranteed rate, tax-deferred. When a family member dies, the death benefit—tax-free—replenishes the bank for the next generation. It is a closed-loop system of perpetual wealth.

How You Can Start

You don't need a billion dollars to start your own Rockefeller Method. You just need the right structure and a commitment to your children's children. It starts with a single policy and a well-drafted trust.

In my practice, I guide families through the technical engineering of these policies, ensuring they are optimized for cash accumulation rather than just death benefit protection.
